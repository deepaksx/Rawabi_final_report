Production Planning — Demand Planning

Production is planned on raw milk volume (liters) instead of milk components (fat, protein, lactose) — a critical architectural gap in dairy manufacturing. All demand forecasting, milk balancing, and fat allocation are performed entirely in Excel by individuals using tribal knowledge; SAP is not utilized for the most critical planning decisions. No statistical forecasting tools, no MAPE/bias tracking, and seasonal patterns (Ramadan +60%) rely on personal experience rather than system intelligence.
